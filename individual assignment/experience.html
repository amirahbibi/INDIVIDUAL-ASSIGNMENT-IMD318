Internship Experience

During my two-month internship at the Pahang State Archive, I gained invaluable experience in archiving and preservation, including cataloging and organizing historical documents, assisting with digitization projects, and supporting researchers in accessing records. I was introduced to archive management systems, learned about legal and ethical practices, and contributed to ongoing projects and exhibitions. The internship enhanced my problem-solving skills as I handled fragile materials and incomplete records while collaborating with archivists and historians. This experience deepened my understanding of the critical role archives play in preserving history and cultural heritage.

  1. Record preservation process
  2. The process of transferring records from the old box to the new box
  3. Records cleaning process
  4. Write a Record minutes
  5. How to locate record in storage room
